Fried Rice


Ingredients

  • Day old rice
  • Eggs
  • Frozen Mixed Veggies
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Neutral Oil
  • Laoganma Fried Chili in Oil
  • White pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan with neutral oil. Add in rice and smush down. Leave untouched until a little crispy.
  2. Deglaze with soy sauce and mirin. Add frozen veggies.
  3. Push this mixture to the side of the pan and cook scrambled eggs in the other side.
  4. Stir to combine. Add in seasonings like chili oil, white pepper, salt.
